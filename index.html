About Test

The tests we conduct include questions from all subjects based on the topics taught during the week. Test's are designed in such a way to prevent copying, cause each test will have a time limit and timer will start as soon as you start test, You can't copy if you try, you'll have less time to attempt the remaining questions due to very strict time limit. Test scores are used for ranking and points are awarded accordingly.

How ranks are assigned?

The top 3 scorer's of test are assigned medal's accordingly, each medal have points such as

= 30 pts

= 20 pts

= 10 pts

In case if two students score 29, the one who finishes faster will be ranked higher.
